Our Ecosystem Vision

First Runner isn't just a hackathon project; it's designed from the ground up to be a sustainable, scalable business model across four strategic phases.

1

Phase 1: Free Tier & Acquisition

Launch with a powerful free version featuring basic audio coaching and tracking to aggressively capture market share and user data.

2

Phase 2: First Runner PRO (Recurring Rev)

Premium subscription unlocking full Gemini Vision API capabilities: frame-by-frame postural analysis, custom routines, and uninterrupted multimodal interaction.

3

Phase 3: Hardware Integrations

Sync with Wear OS, Apple Watch, and smart footwear. Creating a lock-in effect where our AI becomes the brain of their existing physical devices.

4

Phase 4: B2B Coaches & Gyms

Licensing the First Runner platform for personal trainers and elite gyms to manage their clients remotely with AI-assisted insights and white-label reporting.
