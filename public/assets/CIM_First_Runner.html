Confidential Information Memorandum (CIM)

Project Name: First Runner
Date: April 2026

1. Executive Summary

First Runner is an innovative, cross-platform fitness application that bridges the gap between physical health, artificial intelligence, and immersive gamification mechanics. Designed as a "Cyberpunk-themed Turf War," the application transforms daily outdoor runs into an immersive, localized competitive experience.

Powered by Google's Gemini 2.5 Flash Native, First Runner features a state-of-the-art AI Coach (The Oracle) capable of zero-latency, context-aware audio interactions. Users run to capture real-world hexagonal territories (Turfs) while earning virtual currency ('Energy Cells') and cosmetic rewards.

Status: The V2 Protocol is currently deployed, marking a major architectural leap with advanced AI audio capabilities. It is positioned as a strong contender in the Gemini Live Agent Challenge 2026.

2. The Problem & Solution

The Problem: Traditional running apps (Strava, Nike Run Club) rely on static metric tracking, leading to high user churn and a lack of real-time emotional engagement. Furthermore, fitness tracking rarely provides dynamic, in-the-moment personalized coaching.

The Solution: First Runner introduces an AI-driven gamified layer. Instead of running for stats, users run to "conquer" real-world map grids. The AI Coach provides real-time tactical advice based on GPS location and pace, actively conversing with the runner with sub-second latency. User engagement is retained through territorial defense, social "Guild" leaderboards, and an immersive virtual economy.

3. Product & Technology

First Runner is built on a robust, highly scalable, modern technology stack:

  • Mobile Client: Flutter (Cross-platform iOS & Android), featuring a 55-screen comprehensive UI with a premium Dark Mode/Neon aesthetic.
  • AI Engine (The Oracle): Native implementation of Gemini 2.5 Flash Native Audio. Features "Zero-Latency Barge-in", allowing runners to interrupt the AI coach seamlessly using AudioContextAndroid without pausing the microphone.
  • Backend & Cloud: Node.js backend hosted on Google Cloud Run utilizing raw WebSocket PCM for audio streaming.
  • Database & Auth: Firebase ecosystem (Auth, Firestore for real-time territorial data, FCM for invasion alerts).
  • Geolocation Engine: Real-time background GPS tracking via background services, injecting spatial telemetry into the AI's prompt context. H3 Hexagon grid system.
  • Virtual Economy Engine: Native in-app balance system for 'Energy Cells' and cosmetic marketplace.

4. Market Opportunity

The intersection of the Health & Fitness App market, AI Coaching, and GameFi is experiencing explosive growth. First Runner targets:

  • Casual & Hardcore Runners: Looking for increased motivation through gamification.
  • Tech Enthusiasts & Gamers: Drawn to the Cyberpunk aesthetic and "Turf War" mechanics.
  • Move-to-Earn Enthusiasts: Interested in earning rewards through real-world physical activity.

5. Competitive Advantage

  • Context-Aware AI: Unlike generic audio cues, the Gemini integration knows the user's exact pace and whether they are approaching a rival territory.
  • Always-On Gamification: The map tracker operates transparently in the background, pinging real-time push notifications of invasions.
  • Aesthetic & Immersion: High-fidelity tactical HUD.

6. Monetization Strategy

First Runner is engineered for multiple revenue streams:

  • Freemium SaaS Model: Basic metric tracking is free. Premium subscriptions unlock advanced AI Oracle analytics and unlimited interactive voice minutes.
  • In-App Purchases / Marketplace: Cosmetic digital assets (Urban Skins, VFX trails) for runner avatars and territory markers.
  • B2B Geo-Ads: Local businesses sponsor "Pit Stops" during missions.

7. Development Roadmap

  • Phase 1 (Current): V2 Deployment, AI Audio stabilization, Core Turf War mechanics.
  • Phase 2: iOS App Store optimization and official multi-platform synced launch.
  • Phase 3: Advanced Guild/Clan wars mechanics with localized worldwide leaderboards.
  • Phase 4: Full deployment of virtual economy, Battle Pass system, and cosmetic marketplace for distributed rewards.
